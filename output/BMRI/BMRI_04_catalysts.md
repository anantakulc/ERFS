1. **FY26 dividend declaration (~Rp 490–500 DPS, ~7.7%+ yield)** — AGM ~Mar 2026; confirmation of sustained ~78% payout re-anchors the income thesis and is the single highest-probability near-term positive.
2. **2Q26 results confirming organic loan growth at/above 7–9%** — proves the franchise is not just a BRIS base effect.
3. **NIM stabilisation at/above 4.7%** — asset-yield repricing on higher IDR govvies offsetting funding cost; beats the cut 4.5–4.7% guide.
4. **Sustained sub-1% gross NPL with declining cost of credit** — quality-of-earnings beat, the strongest BMRI differentiator.
5. **Livin'/Kopra monetisation milestones** — fee income and CASA growth, structural operating leverage.
6. **BI rate-cut pivot** if IDR stabilises sub-Rp 16,500 — directly compresses CoE and re-rates P/BV.
7. **Danantara dividend clarity favouring high payout** — if the sovereign fund formalises BMRI as a cash-cow dividend payer rather than a capital-redeployment vehicle.
8. **MSCI EM status retained** — removes forced-selling overhang; relief rally.
9. **GGM re-rating toward 1.4–1.5x** as governance discount narrows on Danantara transparency.
10. **Wholesale pipeline delivery** — FDI/downstreaming (nickel), infrastructure, data-centre lending converting to drawn loans in 2H26.
