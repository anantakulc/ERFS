## Bear Case

At 64.1× trailing GAAP P/E and 44.7× EV/EBITDA, Broadcom has zero tolerance for execution failures. The three hyperscaler XPU customers (Google, Meta, Microsoft) are simultaneously qualifying alternatives — Marvell for Azure Maia, Google's internal TPU team, Meta's silicon independence programme. Each XPU design cycle (2–3 years) is a competitive re-tender with no binding revenue guarantee. If one defects, FY2027E EPS collapses from $19.32 toward $10–12; at 20× that is $200–240 — a 38–48% drawdown from current. VMware recovery requires >85% enterprise retention in a market where customer satisfaction data post-acquisition remains strained. Near-term EPS revision breadth is net negative (0q ratio: 0.44; +1q: 0.38), and a Q3 FY2026 miss would trigger a disproportionate sell reaction as bears use it to challenge the FY2027 consensus model.
