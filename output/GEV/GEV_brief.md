## Bear Case
GEV trades at ~37.5x forward EPS and ~2.8x forward SOTP for a margin-expansion-and-supercycle outcome that is largely consensus. Three highest-conviction thesis-breakers: (1) **Valuation** — DCF (~$419) and SOTP (~$333) sit near half the price, so a flawless outcome still offers thin margin of safety; (2) **Earnings quality** — FY25 GAAP net income ($4.88B) and FY26E EPS (~$31) are inflated by one-time/tax items, with operating income a fraction of net income (Q1'26: $179M op vs $4.75B net); (3) **Premium compression** — a ~47% multiple premium to peers re-rates fast on any growth wobble, and the stock is already −22% from its 52-week high.

- Crowded thematic positioning (79.6% institutional) unwinding into a macro/AI-capex scare.
